Argentine soybeans were steady to firm in moderate volume cash market trade midsession Tuesday at the Buenos Aires Cereals Exchange , traders said . 
the market was fairly quiet in the absence of big movements in Chicago , although attention was focussed on the progressing harvest to see if yields are as bad as rumored . 
the spot price paid to soybean suppliers in Rosario was unchanged at $ 293 . 
may sunflower seed firmed $ 2 in moderate volume to $ 222 , fed by demand from crushers looking to meet oil export committments . 
spot Buenos Aires maize was unchanged at $ 110 in good volume trade . 
Bahia Blanca wheat firmed $ 2 to $ 158 .
