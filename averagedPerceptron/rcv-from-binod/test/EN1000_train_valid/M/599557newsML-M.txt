Shanghai soybean futures ended mixed in slow trading on Tuesday on a lack of market-moving news , traders said . 
the most actively traded November 1997 contract ended at 3,348 yuan ( $ 404 ) per tonne , losing 33 yuan . 
it opened at 3,380 yuan , hitting an intraday high of 3,390 and a low of 3,346 . 
July fell 47 to 3,295 , September rose five to 3,386 , the January 1998 contract gained two yuan to 3,382 and March rose five yuan to 3,385 yuan per tonne . 
combined volume fell to 15,000 tonnes from 16,200 tonnes . 
soybeans opened mixed and moved around opening levels for most of the session , traders said .
