the Republic of Venezuela will issue a seven-year , 650 million-mark Eurobond , probably on Thursday , lead manager Westdeutsche Landesbank said on Monday . 
the bond will carry a coupon of 10.25 or 10.50 percent and yield 430 to 450 basis points above comparable German government paper , WestLB said . 
the bond will be the fifth mark-denominated Eurobond issued by Venezuela .
